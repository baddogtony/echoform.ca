Based in Hay River, Northwest Territories, Echo Form Studio looks to deliver exceptional value to small businesses and organizations. No matter the scale of the project, we're always looking for innovative ways to make a positive impact in our own communities and beyond.
At the heart of our work is a commitment to building lasting partnerships. We thrive on close collaboration – using our team’s flexibility to discover fresh, innovative ideas that solve challenges and bring projects to life.

Website, Branding & Digital Support
Foxfire Collective, 2025
Foxfire Collective, an EDI-focused consultancy specializing in organizational transformation, required a visual identity built entirely from the ground up. We engaged in a deep collaboration to define exactly who they are, and subsequently created all the necessary assets to effectively project that identity to the world.
Branding & Graphic Design
Hay River Fire Department, 2026
Celebrating 75 years of operation, the Hay River Fire Department is a historic, volunteer-based organization dedicated to growing its committed membership. We assisted them by standardizing their brand assets and enhancing their recruiting tools, significantly strengthening their visual presence within the communities they proudly serve.
Branding
Sustainable Oceans Conference, 2023
Bringing together academics, practitioners, and the public for interdisciplinary collaboration, the Sustainable Ocean Conference stands as Atlantic Canada's only student-led event of its kind. Based on 2023’s "Turning Tides" theme, we created a compelling visual identity, ensuring the conference achieved immediate recognition and strong engagement across its wide audience.
